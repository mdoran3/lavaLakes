Erta Ale, Ethiopia

Erta Ale

Erta Ale is a continuously flowing basaltic shield volcano in the Afar Region of northeastern Ethiopia. The area is known as the Danakil Depression, a very low lying desert area where Earth's crust is quite literally tearing at its seams. Erta Ale forms part of the East African Rift, a tectonic plate boundary.
