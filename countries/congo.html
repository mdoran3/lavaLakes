Nyiragongo, Democratic Republic of Congo

Nyiragongo Volcano

Located on the eastern border of the Democratic Republic of Congo, Nyiragongo is the largest persistent lava lake in the world.

***With a depth of 600 meters, Nyiragongo is one of the most active volcanoes in the world.
