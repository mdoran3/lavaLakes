Masaya

Masaya is a caldera with one of the most active volcanoes in the country. It sits only 12 miles away from Nicaragua's capital city, Managua. While Masaya is active and remains in motion, it is relatively calm compared to other lava lakes. Nevertheless, It has seen some explosions and lava flows outside of its usualy confinements in the past century. It produced two much larger events recorded in the 17th and 18th centuries.
